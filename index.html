Fruit's Basket

April 5, 2019

A cliche, standard, and generic shoujo. However, it is done good so far. I tend to cringe and the stereotypical shoujo moments, but it kept me intrigued and wanting more. I guess I'll start trying to take pointers from these guys because these girls eat them up haha. It's always funny to me when popular characters have a fan-club in the school. Anyway, it decent time, worth a watch if you need a little romance anime in your life.

Hitori Bocchi

April 5, 2019

As someone who has suffered from extreme social anxiety, it's always nice to see you're not the only one with mind-boggling self conciousness. Bocchi is an adorable air-head and we must protecc. Shows like this might be a little cringe, but they are loveable, and that's all I need. It encourages me to try to make new friends.
